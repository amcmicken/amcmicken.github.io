Where You Are

Where You Are is an interesting site that is promoting a book including 16 artists, writers, thinkers, and their personal maps. The site is set up horizontally and breaks the edges of the browser. You are encouraged to click and drag around to explore everything. What I found most intriguiging is the fact that you can see where someone else is reading because a yellow bordered box pops up. I think that is such an interesting and fitting idea for this particular work.
